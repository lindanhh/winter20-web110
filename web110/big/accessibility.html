Accessibility

Back then when web pages were only used for information, users did not have a way of contacting or communicating with the owner or developer of the web page. Now with the significant increases of use of technology, feedback from users are very beneficial. The feedback is received from a web form. “A web form is a medium that allows your visitors to get in contact with you and to send information, such as an order, a catalog request, or even a query, which is passed on to your database.” You are able to create basic forms with no styling to get the point across or have forms that have styling to make it more appealing to users. You’re able to create forms that have certain requirements for each text box like a min or max required character count or certain special characters. We see web forms on pages more often than you think. Online logins is a huge web form that has great uses.
