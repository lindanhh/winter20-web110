Social Media Optimization (SMO)

When having a web page, something called social media optimization is critical in getting you points in the popularity contest. According to Wikipedia, Social media optimization (SMO) is the use of a number of outlets and communities to generate publicity to increase the awareness of a product, service brand or event.” We can’t always count on users to find your website on the Web by search, you need to promote your web page to get more exposure. Whether your web page is for information of a social page, interaction is a key part in getting your web page out for others to see. It’s important to have a strategic way in getting the correct exposure for your web page.

Key Points of SMO

  • SMO is important to spread awareness of your webpage
  • Small tweaks of optimization can make a big difference in exposure for your webpage
